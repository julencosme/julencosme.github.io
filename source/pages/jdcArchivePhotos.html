IA Studio | NOLA DNA

IA Studio & NOLA DNA is a monumental operation located in New Orleans, Louisiana. The archive and print studio houses an extensive collection of the Times-Picayune, from 1885 up to 1930. This grand enterprise is owned, operated and curated by Joseph Makkos. I had the great opportunity, as a volunteer, to aid the CEO with tasks such as helping to organize the Harper's Weekly Magazine.
