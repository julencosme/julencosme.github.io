IA Studio | NOLA DNA

IA Studio & NOLA DNA is a monumental operation located in New Orleans, Louisiana. The archive and print studio houses an extensive contiguous collection of the Times-Picayune, spanning 1885 to 1930. This grand enterprise is owned, operated, and curated by Joseph Makkos. I had the great opportunity, as a volunteer, to aid the CEO with tasks such as helping to organize Harper's Weekly magazine.
